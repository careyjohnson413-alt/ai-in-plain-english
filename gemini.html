What Is Gemini?

Gemini is Google's AI assistant. It used to be called "Bard," but Google renamed it and built it directly into the tools you probably already use — like Google Search, Gmail, and Google Docs.

What Can You Use It For?

  • Asking questions right inside Google Search
  • Drafting and editing emails in Gmail
  • Writing documents and presentations in Google Workspace
  • Analyzing images and photos you upload
  • Planning trips, comparing products, and researching topics

Is It Free?

The basic version is free with any Google account. Google One AI Premium (about $20/month) gives you access to the most powerful version and integrates it into Gmail, Docs, and more.

How Do You Get Started?

If you have a Google account, go to gemini.google.com and start chatting. You can also find it built into Google Search — just look for the AI-generated summaries at the top of your search results.

What Makes It Different?

Gemini's biggest advantage is that it's woven into Google's ecosystem. If you already live in Gmail, Google Docs, and Google Search, Gemini meets you where you are instead of making you go to a separate website.
