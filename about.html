About

Why this blog exists, who it's for, and what you'll find here.

Our mission

AI in Plain English exists for one reason: to make artificial intelligence approachable for everyone who isn't an engineer. No jargon. No gatekeeping. No assuming you already know what "LLM," "prompt engineering," or "multimodal" mean.

If you've ever heard a friend rave about ChatGPT and thought, "that sounds useful, but where do I even start?" — this site is built for you.

What you'll find here

  • Tool guides — plain-English overviews of ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and the other major AI tools, including how to sign up, what they cost, and what they're best at.
  • New-release explainers — when a major model drops (like GPT Image 2 or Claude Opus 4.7), the blog covers it in everyday language shortly after.
  • Use-case breakdowns — realistic interpretations of what each tool is designed to do, what it's well-suited for, and where it's likely to fall short.
  • How-tos — step-by-step walkthroughs for common tasks like writing better prompts, making images, summarizing documents, or generating video.
  • A built-in chatbot — every page has an "Ask" button in the corner. Type any AI question and it'll answer using information from the posts on this site.

About this site

AI in Plain English is an independent, reader-focused blog. It is not sponsored by any AI company, and it does not take affiliate commissions on the tools it covers. Posts describe what tools are designed to do based on public information and vendor documentation — they do not invent experiences or results.

Our principles

If your grandmother couldn't follow it, it's not plain English yet.
  • No jargon without a definition. Every technical term is explained the first time it appears.
  • Honest about limits. Every tool has flaws — hallucinations, privacy trade-offs, cost, ethics. These are called out alongside the capabilities.
  • No fabricated anecdotes. When a post describes a use case, it is presented as an interpretation of what the tool enables — not as a personal story that didn't happen.
  • Fast, not flashy. The site is lightweight on purpose. No tracking cookies, no pop-ups, no autoplay anything.
  • Updated as things change. AI moves fast. When a tool changes meaningfully, the relevant post is updated.

Get in touch

Spot an error? Have a tool you want covered? Tap the chat button in the bottom-right on any page to ask a question, or reach out directly.
