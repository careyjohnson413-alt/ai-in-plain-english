What Is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic, a company focused on making AI that's safe and helpful. Claude is known for giving thoughtful, nuanced answers — especially for longer conversations and writing projects.

What Can You Use It For?

  • Writing and editing essays, articles, and creative pieces
  • Having in-depth conversations about complex topics
  • Analyzing documents and PDFs you upload
  • Summarizing long reports or meeting notes
  • Learning about unfamiliar subjects at your own pace

Is It Free?

Yes — there's a free tier at claude.ai. The Pro plan (about $20/month) gives you more usage and access to the most capable model.

How Do You Get Started?

Go to claude.ai, sign up with your email, and start typing. The interface is clean and simple — just a text box and a conversation.

What Makes It Different?

Claude tends to be more careful and less likely to make things up compared to some other AI tools. It's also great at following detailed instructions, which makes it especially useful for writing projects where you want a specific tone or structure.
