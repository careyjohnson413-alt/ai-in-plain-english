What Is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot made by a company called OpenAI. You type a question or request into a text box, and it writes back in natural, conversational English. It launched in November 2022 and quickly became the fastest-growing app in history.

What Can You Use It For?

  • Drafting emails, letters, or messages
  • Brainstorming ideas for gifts, meals, or projects
  • Summarizing long articles
  • Explaining topics in simple terms
  • Helping with homework or research

Is It Free?

Yes — there's a free version that uses an older model. The paid plan (ChatGPT Plus, about $20/month) gives you access to the latest, smartest model and extra features like image generation.

How Do You Get Started?

Go to chat.openai.com, create a free account with your email address, and start typing. That's it — no downloads, no installations.

Anything to Watch Out For?

ChatGPT sometimes makes things up (called "hallucinations"). It can sound very confident while being completely wrong, so always double-check important facts, especially medical, legal, or financial information.
